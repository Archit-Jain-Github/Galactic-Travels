7-Day Neptune Tour Itinerary

Day 1:

  • Arrive at Neptune International Spaceport and check into your hotel.
  • Take a guided tour of the spaceport and learn about the history of Neptune exploration.
  • Have dinner at one of the spaceport's many restaurants, offering stunning views of Neptune and its moons.

Day 2:

  • Visit the Neptune Museum and learn about the planet's unique geology, climate, and atmosphere.
  • Take a boat tour of Triton, Neptune's largest moon, and see its active geysers and frozen lakes.
  • Have dinner at a restaurant on Triton, where you can enjoy fresh seafood and local delicacies.

Day 3:

  • Visit the Neptune Space Observatory and learn about the planet's many rings and moons.
  • Take a spacewalk to Proteus, Neptune's second largest moon, and explore its unusual potato-shaped surface.
  • Have dinner at a restaurant on Proteus, where you can enjoy panoramic views of Neptune and its rings.

Day 4:

  • Visit the Neptune Research Facility and learn about the latest scientific discoveries about the planet.
  • Take a submarine tour of Neptune's Great Dark Spot, a massive storm system that has been raging for over a century.
  • Have dinner at a restaurant on the research facility, where you can enjoy a meal with Neptune scientists and engineers.

Day 5:

  • Visit the Neptune Space Academy and learn about the history of space travel and the future of Neptune exploration.
  • Take a flight simulator ride and experience the thrill of piloting a spacecraft through Neptune's rings.
  • Have dinner at a restaurant in the space academy, where you can enjoy a meal with other space tourists from around the world.

Day 6:

  • Visit the Neptune Memorial Wall and pay your respects to the astronauts and scientists who have lost their lives in the exploration of Neptune.
  • Take a final walk around Neptune International Spaceport and enjoy the stunning views of Neptune and its moons.
  • Have dinner at one of the spaceport's many restaurants, where you can reflect on your amazing experience on Neptune.

Day 7:

  • Depart from Neptune International Spaceport and return to Earth.
