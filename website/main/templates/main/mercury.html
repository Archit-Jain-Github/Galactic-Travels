7-Day Mercury Tour Itinerary

Day 1:

  • Arrive at the Mercury Spaceport and check into your hotel.
  • Have lunch at the spaceport cafeteria.
  • Take a guided tour of the spaceport facilities.
  • Meet your fellow tour members and tour guide.
  • Have dinner at the spaceport restaurant.

Day 2:

  • Take a rocket shuttle to the surface of Mercury.
  • Arrive at the Caloris Basin, a giant impact crater that covers nearly one-third of the planet's surface.
  • Explore the basin's rim and learn about its formation.
  • Have lunch at a picnic site overlooking the basin.
  • Visit the Mariner 10 Memorial, a plaque commemorating the first spacecraft to visit Mercury.
  • Return to the rocket shuttle and travel back to the spaceport.
  • Have dinner at the spaceport restaurant.

Day 3:

  • Visit the Mercury Museum, which houses a collection of artifacts and exhibits related to the planet's exploration.
  • Take a virtual tour of the planet's surface.
  • Learn about Mercury's unique environment, including its extreme temperature fluctuations and lack of atmosphere.
  • Have lunch at the museum cafeteria.
  • Visit the Mercury Space Observatory, where you can learn about the planet's astronomy and see it through a telescope.
  • Have dinner at the spaceport restaurant.

Day 4:

  • Take a rocket shuttle to the Caloris Basin again.
  • Hike to the bottom of the basin and explore its features, such as impact craters and lava flows.
  • Have lunch at a picnic site at the bottom of the basin.
  • Visit the Mercury Volcano Observatory, where you can learn about the planet's volcanic activity and see active lava flows.
  • Return to the rocket shuttle and travel back to the spaceport.
  • Have dinner at the spaceport restaurant.

Day 5:

  • Visit the Mercury Spaceport Visitor Center, where you can see exhibits on the planet's exploration and learn about the latest space missions.
  • Take a virtual tour of the solar system.
  • Have lunch at the visitor center cafeteria.
  • Visit the Mercury Spaceport Gift Shop, where you can purchase souvenirs and memorabilia.
  • Have dinner at the spaceport restaurant.

Day 6:

  • Take a rocket shuttle to the Mercury Spaceport's launch pad.
  • Board a spacecraft for your journey back to Earth.
  • Say goodbye to your fellow tour members and tour guide.
  • Enjoy the view of Mercury from space as you depart.

Day 7:

  • Arrive back at Earth and disembark from the spacecraft.
  • Travel back to your home destination.
