7-Day Tour to Uranus

Day 1:

  • Arrive at Uranus Spaceport and check into your hotel.
  • Have a welcome dinner with your tour group and tour guide.
  • Receive a briefing on the safety procedures and itinerary for the tour.

Day 2:

  • Visit the Uranus Museum to learn about the planet's history, geology, and atmosphere.
  • Take a tour of the Uranus Spaceport and learn about the latest space exploration technology.
  • Have lunch at the Spaceport Cafe.
  • Take a flight to the Miranda moon of Uranus.
  • Explore the Miranda moon's unique geological features, including its canyons, craters, and ridges.
  • Have dinner at the Miranda Moon Base.

Day 3:

  • Take a flight to the Ariel moon of Uranus.
  • Explore the Ariel moon's icy surface and its many craters.
  • Visit the Ariel Moon Observatory and learn about the latest astronomical research on Uranus and its moons.
  • Have lunch at the Ariel Moon Base.
  • Take a flight back to Uranus.
  • Have dinner at your hotel.

Day 4:

  • Take a flight to the Umbriel moon of Uranus.
  • Explore the Umbriel moon's dark surface and its many craters.
  • Visit the Umbriel Moon Cave and learn about the moon's unique geology.
  • Have lunch at the Umbriel Moon Base.
  • Take a flight back to Uranus.
  • Have dinner at your hotel.

Day 5:

  • Take a flight to the Titania moon of Uranus.
  • Explore the Titania moon's icy surface and its many canyons and valleys.
  • Visit the Titania Moon Observatory and learn about the latest astronomical research on Uranus and its moons.
  • Have lunch at the Titania Moon Base.
  • Take a flight back to Uranus.
  • Have dinner at your hotel.

Day 6:

  • Visit the Uranus Rings Observatory and learn about the planet's unique ring system.
  • Take a flight through the Uranus rings and experience the stunning views of the planet and its moons.
  • Have lunch at the Uranus Rings Space Station.
  • Return to Uranus Spaceport.
  • Have a farewell dinner with your tour group and tour guide.

Day 7:

  • Depart from Uranus Spaceport.
