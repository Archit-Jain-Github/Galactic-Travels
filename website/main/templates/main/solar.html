7-Day Complete Solar System Tour

Day 1:

  • Arrive at the Space Tours and Travels spaceport and board your starship.
  • Meet your tour guide and fellow passengers.
  • Depart for the Sun.
  • Arrive at the Sun and orbit from a safe distance.
  • Witness the Sun's incredible power and beauty.
  • Return to your starship and travel to Mercury.

Day 2:

  • Arrive at Mercury and land on its surface.
  • Explore the planet's unique features, such as its craters, cliffs, and valleys.
  • Visit the Mariner 10 memorial site.
  • Return to your starship and travel to Venus.

Day 3:

  • Arrive at Venus and enter its orbit.
  • Observe the planet's thick atmosphere and volcanic activity.
  • Visit the Magellan spacecraft memorial site.
  • Return to your starship and travel to Earth.

Day 4:

  • Arrive at Earth and land at the Space Tours and Travels spaceport.
  • Visit the International Space Station (ISS) and take a tour of its facilities.
  • Conduct space experiments and learn about life in space.
  • Return to your starship and travel to the Moon.

Day 5:

  • Arrive at the Moon and land on its surface.
  • Explore the Moon's craters, mountains, and valleys.
  • Visit the Apollo 11 memorial site.
  • Return to your starship and travel to Mars.

Day 6:

  • Arrive at Mars and land on its surface.
  • Explore the planet's canyons, valleys, and volcanoes.
  • Visit the Opportunity rover memorial site.
  • Return to your starship and travel to the outer solar system.

Day 7:

  • Arrive at the outer solar system and visit the moons of Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune.
  • Observe the Great Red Spot on Jupiter.
  • See the rings of Saturn up close.
  • Visit the Miranda ice cliffs on Uranus.
  • See the Great Dark Spot on Neptune.
  • Return to the Space Tours and Travels spaceport and disembark from your starship.
