7-Day Space Adventure in Earth Orbit

Day 1:

  • Arrive at the launch site and check into your crew quarters.
  • Meet your fellow crew members and the tour staff.
  • Receive a briefing on the safety procedures and the itinerary for the week.
  • Suit up and board the spacecraft.
  • Launch into space!

Day 2:

  • Arrive at the International Space Station (ISS).
  • Dock with the ISS and board.
  • Give yourself a tour of the ISS and meet the resident crew.
  • Conduct scientific experiments or perform maintenance tasks on the ISS.
  • Enjoy your first meal in space!

Day 3:

  • Take a spacewalk and experience the weightlessness of space.
  • Float around the ISS and admire the views of Earth.
  • Collect samples or take photographs.
  • Return to the ISS and rest up for another day of adventure.

Day 4:

  • Learn about the history of the space program and the ISS.
  • Visit the ISS museum and see artifacts from past missions.
  • Talk to the resident crew about their experiences living and working in space.
  • Enjoy a meal with the ISS crew.

Day 5:

  • Perform more scientific experiments or maintenance tasks.
  • Conduct educational outreach activities for students on Earth.
  • Participate in a video conference with your family and friends.
  • Relax and enjoy your time in space.

Day 6:

  • Prepare to leave the ISS.
  • Undock from the ISS and board the spacecraft.
  • Wave goodbye to the resident crew and begin your journey back to Earth.

Day 7:

  • Land safely back at the launch site.
  • Debrief with the tour staff and share your experiences with your fellow crew members.
  • Say goodbye to your new friends and return to Earth!
