7-Day Moon Trip Itinerary

Day 1:

  • Arrive at the launch facility and check in for your flight.
  • Attend a pre-flight briefing with your mission commander and crew mates.
  • Board the spacecraft and blast off for the Moon!

Day 2:

  • Enjoy the journey to the Moon. Take in the stunning views of Earth and the solar system from your window seat.
  • Participate in scientific experiments and training exercises.
  • Relax and get to know your fellow crew members.

Day 3:

  • Arrive at the Moon and enter lunar orbit.
  • Prepare for your descent to the surface.
  • Land on the Moon!

Day 4:

  • Take your first steps on the Moon!
  • Explore the lunar surface and collect samples.
  • Conduct scientific experiments.
  • Set up camp for the night.

Day 5:

  • Continue your exploration of the Moon.
  • Visit the Apollo 11 landing site.
  • Take a lunar rover ride.
  • Enjoy a picnic lunch on the Moon!

Day 6:

  • Prepare for your return to Earth.
  • Board the spacecraft and lift off from the Moon.
  • Enjoy the journey back home.

Day 7:

  • Arrive at the launch facility and disembark from the spacecraft.
  • Say goodbye to your crew mates and mission commander.
  • Celebrate your successful mission to the Moon!
