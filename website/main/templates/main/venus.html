7-Day Venus Tour

Day 1:

  • Arrive at Venus Spaceport and check into your hotel.
  • Take a guided tour of the spaceport and learn about the history of Venus exploration.
  • Have dinner at one of the many restaurants on the spaceport.

Day 2:

  • Take a shuttle to Aphrodite Terra, the largest continent on Venus.
  • Visit the Ovda Regio volcano, one of the largest volcanoes in the solar system.
  • Have lunch at a picnic spot overlooking the volcano.
  • In the afternoon, visit the Lada Terra region, which is home to a variety of geological features, including lava flows, craters, and mountains.
  • Have dinner at a restaurant in the Lada Terra region.

Day 3:

  • Take a shuttle to Ishtar Terra, the second-largest continent on Venus.
  • Visit the Maxwell Montes mountain range, the highest mountain range in the solar system.
  • Have lunch at a picnic spot overlooking the mountains.
  • In the afternoon, visit the Lakshmi Planum plateau, which is home to a variety of geological features, including valleys, canyons, and plains.
  • Have dinner at a restaurant in the Lakshmi Planum region.

Day 4:

  • Take a shuttle to the clouds of Venus.
  • Board a cloud-exploration balloon and take a tour of the Venusian atmosphere.
  • Have lunch on board the balloon.
  • In the afternoon, return to the surface of Venus.
  • Have dinner at a restaurant on the spaceport.

Day 5:

  • Visit the Venus Science Center and learn about the latest scientific research on Venus.
  • Take a guided tour of the Venus Botanical Garden, which features plants that have been adapted to the Venusian environment.
  • Have lunch at a restaurant in the botanical garden.
  • In the afternoon, visit the Venus Art Gallery, which features art inspired by Venus.
  • Have dinner at a restaurant on the spaceport.

Day 6:

  • Take a shuttle to the Venus Observatory and learn about the latest astronomical research on Venus.
  • Take a guided tour of the observatory and see the various telescopes and other instruments that are used to study Venus.
  • Have lunch at a restaurant in the observatory.
  • In the afternoon, return to the spaceport.
  • Have dinner at a restaurant on the spaceport.

Day 7:

  • Depart from Venus Spaceport.
