7-Day Sun Tour

Day 1:

- Arrive at the Space Tours and Travels launch facility and board your spacecraft.
- Meet your crew and fellow passengers, and receive a safety briefing.
- Launch into space and begin your journey to the Sun.

Day 2:

- Enjoy stunning views of the Earth and Moon as you travel through space.
- Learn about the Sun and its importance to our solar system from your expert crew.
- Participate in hands-on experiments and activities related to the Sun.

Day 3:

- Arrive at a safe distance from the Sun and begin your observations.
- Use state-of-the-art telescopes and other instruments to study the Sun's surface, atmosphere, and corona.
- Learn about the latest solar research and discoveries from your crew.

Day 4:

- Continue your observations of the Sun, focusing on different features and phenomena.
- Participate in a live Q&A session with a solar scientist on Earth.
- Enjoy a special spacewalk to experience the Sun's heat and energy firsthand.

Day 5:

- Begin your journey back to Earth.
- Review the data and images you collected during your tour, and prepare your presentation for the other passengers.
- Participate in a farewell dinner and celebrate the success of your mission.

Day 6:

- Arrive back at the Space Tours and Travels launch facility and disembark from your spacecraft.
- Share your experiences and findings with the other passengers and the media.
- Receive a certificate of completion for your Sun tour.

Day 7:

- Depart from the Space Tours and Travels launch facility and return home.
