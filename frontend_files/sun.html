1-Month Sun Tour

Day 1:

Arrive at the Spaceport and check into your spacecraft.

Meet your fellow passengers and crew.

Receive a briefing on safety procedures and the itinerary for the trip.

Depart for the Sun!

Days 2-7:

Travel through space, learning about the solar system and the Sun.

Conduct scientific experiments and observations.

Participate in educational activities and workshops.

Enjoy the stunning views of the stars and planets.

Days 8-14:

Arrive at the Sun and enter orbit.

Deploy solar probes and other instruments to study the Sun's surface and atmosphere.

Conduct experiments on the Sun's magnetic field and solar wind.

Capture images and videos of the Sun's corona and other features.

Days 15-21:

Continue to study the Sun and collect data.

Conduct spacewalks to experience the Sun's gravity and heat firsthand.

Learn about the Sun's importance to life on Earth and the solar system.

Participate in public outreach events to share your experience with the world.

Days 22-28:

Depart from the Sun and begin the journey back to Earth.

Analyze the data collected and prepare reports for scientific journals.

Create presentations and educational materials to share your experience with the public.

Enjoy the last few days of space travel!

Day 29:

Arrive back at the Spaceport and disembark from your spacecraft.

Say goodbye to your fellow passengers and crew.

Share your experience with the media and your loved ones.

Day 30:

Depart from the Spaceport and return home.
